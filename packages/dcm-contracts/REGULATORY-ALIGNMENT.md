# Regulatory Alignment - DCM Contracts

This package includes compliance-audit transport contracts that support regulated operational evidence workflows.

## Scope

- Defines request/response structures for compliance audit retrieval and CSV export.
- Covers shape-level interoperability only.

## Non-Goals

- Does not define legal retention periods.
- Does not define jurisdiction-specific compliance policy.
- Does not mandate storage/archive implementation details.

## Intended Use

- Regulated customer audits (for example, healthcare/pharma operational traceability).
- Internal control evidence export for platform governance reviews.
